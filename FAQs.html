FAQs

Everything you need to know about DeenPath

What is DeenPath?

DeenPath is a comprehensive Islamic platform that provides authentic knowledge, prayer times, Quranic resources, and spiritual guidance for Muslims worldwide. We aim to be your trusted companion on your spiritual journey.

Is DeenPath free to use?

Yes, DeenPath offers free access to all its core features including prayer times, Quran reading, duas, and Islamic resources. Some advanced features and courses may require registration.

How accurate are the prayer times?

Our prayer times are calculated using reliable astronomical algorithms based on your location. We use multiple calculation methods to ensure accuracy. You can also customize the calculation method based on your preference.

Do I need to create an account?

While you can browse most content without an account, creating one allows you to save your preferences, bookmark favorite resources, track your progress, and access personalized features.

Which Quran translations are available?

We offer multiple trusted translations including Sahih International, Yusuf Ali, and Pickthall. You can switch between translations easily while reading the Quran.

Is there a mobile app available?

Yes, DeenPath is available as a mobile app for both Android and iOS. You can download it from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store.

How can I contact support?

You can reach out to our support team at mohsingohar759@gmail.com. We typically respond within 24-48 hours. You can also use the contact form on our website.
